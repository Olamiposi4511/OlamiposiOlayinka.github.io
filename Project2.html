Workflow Documentation Process for Lagos State Civil Service Commission

Project Summary

The Workflow Documentation Process project for Lagos State Civil Service Commission began in March 2022 with the objective of improving the transparency, accountability, and efficiency of the organization's services to the public. The project involved documenting the various steps involved in specific tasks or processes within the organization using a systematic approach to identify roles and responsibilities, resources required, and bottlenecks in the process. The Workflow Documentation Process project helped the Lagos State Civil Service Commission to identify and eliminate bottlenecks in their processes, standardize their operations, and improve the quality of service delivery to the public.

Project Objective

  • To improve transparency, accountability, and efficiency in the delivery of services to the public by documenting the various steps involved in specific tasks or processes within the organization.
  • To identify the different steps involved in a particular process, the roles and responsibilities of each stakeholder, and the resources required to complete the task.
  • To document the information in a standardized format, which is easily understood by all stakeholders involved in the process.
  • To identify and eliminate bottlenecks in processes, standardize operations, and improve the quality of service delivery to the public.

Project Scope

The Workflow Documentation Process for Lagos State Civil Service Commission project includes the following activities:

  • Identifying the processes that require documentation
  • Identifying stakeholders involved in the process
  • Documenting the different steps involved in the process
  • Documenting the roles and responsibilities of each stakeholder
  • Identifying the resources required to complete the task
  • Standardizing the documentation format
  • Testing the documentation process
  • Training stakeholders on the new documentation process
  • Implementing the new documentation process
