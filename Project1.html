Tax Computation Application for Kehinde Kassim & Co.

Project Summary

The Tax Computation Application project was initiated in January 2022 to assist Kehinde Kassim & Co. in computing accurate tax liabilities for their clients. The project involved developing a software tool that simplifies the complex tax calculation process by providing a user-friendly interface that allows users to input client data and generate tax computations quickly and accurately. The application was updated with the latest tax laws and regulations, ensuring that the tax computation is compliant with the current tax laws. The application can compute different types of taxes, including income tax, value-added tax (VAT), withholding tax, and other taxes applicable to individuals and businesses.

Project Objective

  • To develop a user-friendly tax computation application for Kehinde Kassim & Co.
  • To ensure that the tax computation application is updated with the latest tax laws and regulations
  • To ensure that the tax computation application can compute different types of taxes, including income tax, value-added tax (VAT), withholding tax, and other taxes applicable to individuals and businesses
  • To streamline the tax computation process for Kehinde Kassim & Co.
  • To improve accuracy and provide clients with timely and reliable tax computations.

Project Scope

The Tax Computation Application project included the following activities:

  • Development of a user-friendly tax computation application for Kehinde Kassim & Co.

  • Providing ongoing maintenance and support to the tax computation application to ensure it remains up-to-date with the latest tax laws and regulations
